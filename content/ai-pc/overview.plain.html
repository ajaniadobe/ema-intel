More than a Great PC. It's an AI PC.

Imagine having a personal assistant who helps you create, collaborate, and complete tasks more easily and efficiently, no matter where you are, while helping keep your data more secure.

Welcome to the next evolution in PCs available now—AI PCs powered by Intel® Core™ Ultra processors. With support for full Copilot+ integration and AI applications and features, Intel-enabled AI PCs help make your life and work easier, giving you more time to do the things you love.

For every way you use your PC, AI PCs are designed for optimal performance, efficiency, reliability, and security. That’s why AI acceleration is built into every Intel® Core™ Ultra processor, with three AI engines—CPU, GPU, and NPU—all working together to support the next era of AI software.

What’s inside:

  • Central processing unit (CPU) for quick response times: This reliable central processor handles general-purpose tasks or sudden voracious workloads.
  • Graphics processing unit (GPU) for high throughput: The GPU excels at large tasks requiring parallel throughput, like rendering visuals, or large AI workloads that need high performance.
  • Neural processing unit (NPU) for power efficiency: An integrated neural processor handles sustained, heavily used AI tasks at lower power for greater efficiency.

AI PCs for Personal Use: What Can You Do with an AI PC?

Create, edit, connect, and play like never before with devices for every budget. Let your Intel® Core™ Ultra processor-based AI PC help you improve and accelerate everyday tasks, such as:

  • Draft emails or organize your calendar.
  • Remix a song with a few clicks and text entries.
  • Generate images or music from text.
  • Enhance photos and videos in a few clicks.
  • Write, code, and create faster with AI assistants.
  • Upscale your games for stunning visuals without sacrificing power or battery.

Compared to AI you use in your web browser, AI PCs powered by Intel® Core™ Ultra processors are able to run AI directly on your PC, making them fast, reliable, and highly efficient.
