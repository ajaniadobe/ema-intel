Widgets?

With widgets, your authors have one more level of nesting without writing complex custom code. Your project can do simple things like place videos next to text inside a columns block. Because widgets are link-based, you can also use them to tokenize complex blocks that may be configured in other apps.

BYO Logging?

Bring your own logging service: Splunk, Coralogix, you name it. Log explicit and implicit errors to keep your site healthy.
