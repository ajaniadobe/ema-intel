Frequently asked questions

Are Intel® Core™ Ultra Series 3 processors a good choice for multiple uses like gaming, working and streaming video?

Intel Core Ultra processors are built to perform across everything you do, from work to gaming and everything in between. With 50% more graphics cores on select SKUs, you get the speed to game, stream and edit media with crisp visuals, or work with robust productivity applications, all without sacrificing performance.

What types of systems and devices feature Intel Core Ultra Series 3 processors?

Intel Core Ultra processors are featured in a wide variety of devices and form-factors, from ultra-slim laptops, powerful 2-in1s that switch between tablet and clamshell, or creator-focused machines with studio-level features. Choose the style that works best for your life without sacrificing performance or battery life whether you’re at home or on the go.

How efficient is battery life for systems featuring Intel Core Ultra Series 3 processors?

Intel Core Ultra processors are designed to be powerful when connected to power or when it matters most: on the move, unplugged and in real life. Intel Evo systems are verified to deliver even longer endurance under real-world conditions using the most widely used games and applications. 8

What AI PC capabilities are included in Intel Core Ultra Series 3 processors?

Intel Core Ultra processors include a number of AI capabilities aimed at improving everyday experiences and promoting battery efficiency. AI capabilities sharpen video calls, improve lighting and block out noise, while speeding up everyday tasks. For gamers and creators, the built-in Intel Arc graphics offer up to 50% more graphics cores on select SKUs, meaning faster, smoother gaming plus AI-accelerated tools.

What kinds of Edge computing applications are best suited for Intel Core Ultra Series 3 processors?

Intel Core Ultra processors for edge are suited for a wide variety of innovative AI use cases, including generative AI (GenAI) and agentic AI, while addressing the unique challenges of the industrial edge. From detecting objects to generating insights and taking autonomous action, the processor brings together classic computer vision, generative AI, and agentic intelligence in a single, power-efficient solution for edge deployments. Additionally, this is the first Intel Core Ultra processor with support for industrial-grade extended temperature.7
