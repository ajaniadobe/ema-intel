Modern Graphics for Everyone

Modernize your graphics experience, with the Intel Arc B580 and Intel Arc B570 GPU, plus a comprehensive collection of software to enhance your experience.

Upgrade Your Gaming Experience

Experience the latest games using the latest tech. From ray-tracing to upscaling, get the performance to handle the latest immersive gaming features and visual effects.

Built for 1440p Gaming

Play many of the latest games at 60+ FPS in 1440p resolution.1

Smoother Experiences

Faster Responsiveness

A hyper-connected gaming experience with Intel® Xe Low Latency (XeLL), which reduces lag and input delays.

Get an AI-Edge in Work and Play2

Advance your creation and play capabilities with exciting new next-gen AI tools and technologies—including upscaled visuals, increased gaming performance, and generative AI capabilities.

Improved Gaming with Intel® XeSS

Get up to 2x performance with high image quality by enabling AI-powered Intel XeSS.1

Powerful AI Engines

Unlock new AI experiences with up to 233 TOPS of AI engine performance for content creation, real-time AI chat, editing, and upscaled gaming.3

Generative AI-ready

Expand Your Content Creativity

Get Cross-Codec Support
